About Us

Rooted in heritage, driven by service

The First Haitian American Lions Club carries the proud legacy of Lions Clubs International into our community — turning kindness into action and neighbors into family.

◎

Our Vision

To be a beacon of hope and unity for Haitian American communities — where every person has the support, dignity, and opportunity to thrive.

✶

Our Mission

To empower volunteers to serve their communities, meet humanitarian needs, and encourage peace and international understanding through Lions clubs.

♥

Core Values

We stand on the pillars that have guided Lions for over a century — living them out in every project we take on.
